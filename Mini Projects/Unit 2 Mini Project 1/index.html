Recipe - Homemade Wonton

Description

Wontons have been something that I’ve always eaten before. This is a traditional Chinese dish, where it is just wrapped dumplings in a soup (usually chicken) and served hot. This is also sold at stores, but I prefer homemade versions of it. It’s a dish that can fill you up and is really good combined with chicken broth. And it’s best served on cold days, where the heat of the soup will fill your tummy up.

The Ingredients that you will need are:
  • Wonton Wrappers
  • Water
  • Meat
  • Chicken broth (or soup of choice)
Instructions
  1. Take the wonton wrappers and insert the meat inside
  2. Take one corner, and wrap it to the other. This forms the corners that will be used
  3. Take the corners and wrap the edges up like a spiral staircase. Then glue it with the water
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 and make more of them
  5. Now you can start preparing the chicken broth, and let it cook it for 5-10 mins
  6. Serve it with the soup in a bowl, and enjoy!
